About Us

We are a multidisiplinary class at Wheaton College, MA working to help people who are diagnosed with diabetes. We are led by Mark LeBlanc who is a Professor of Computer Science. By looking at data given to us by people with diabetus, we are working to create more informative displays of their data to aid them in managing their blood glucose levels over time. Additionally, we are working to plot trends in their respective data with the goal that alerts become more than reactionary, they become predictive.
